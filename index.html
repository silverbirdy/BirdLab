BirdLab is a software studio based in Seoul. We partner with founders and product teams to take ideas from prototype to production — strategy, design, and code, under one roof.

What we do

Product & Strategy

We work with you to define what to build and why. User research, competitive analysis, roadmap prioritization — before a single line of code is written.

Design

End-to-end product design from wireframes to production-ready UI. We build design systems that scale and interfaces that people actually enjoy using.

Engineering

Full-stack development across web, iOS, and backend infrastructure. We write code that ships on time and holds up in production.

AI & Automation

We integrate language models, ML pipelines, and intelligent workflows into products — not as a feature checkbox, but as a genuine advantage.

About

We started BirdLab because we kept seeing the same problem — talented teams with great ideas, stuck in slow execution cycles. Big agencies take months. Freelancers lack breadth. Neither moves at startup speed.

We're small on purpose. A tight team that covers product, design, and engineering means fewer handoffs, faster decisions, and better outcomes. We treat every project like it's our own.
