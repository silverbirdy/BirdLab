Daily Tarot — Privacy Policy

App Name: Daily Tarot
Package Name: com.dailytarot.android
Effective Date: April 19, 2026
Last Updated: April 19, 2026

Daily Tarot ("the App") values your privacy and is committed to protecting your rights under applicable laws. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, phone number, or postal address).

However, to display advertisements, the Google AdMob SDK may collect the following:

  • Advertising ID (Android Advertising ID, ADID) — a resettable identifier provided by Google, used to serve personalized ads.
  • Device Information — device model, OS version, language settings.
  • Approximate Location — derived from IP address for regional ad targeting.

This information is collected and processed by Google AdMob. It is not transmitted to the App developer's servers.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • Personally identifiable information (name, email, phone, address)
  • Precise GPS location
  • Photos, videos, contacts, calendar
  • Voice, health, or financial data
  • User account information (the App has no account system)

3. Information Stored on Your Device

The following information you generate within the App is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to any server:

  • Card draw history (date, category, cards drawn)
  • App settings (reminder time, theme, etc.)
  • Onboarding completion status

This information is permanently deleted when you uninstall the App or choose Settings > Clear History.

4. Purpose of Collection

  • Ad revenue generation via Google AdMob
  • App stability and performance improvements (via Google Play's built-in diagnostics)

5. Third-Party Services

We do not sell or share your information with third parties. However, we use the following third-party service to display ads:

  • Google AdMob — ad delivery and advertising ID processing
    Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. Data Retention

  • On-device data: retained until you delete it yourself
  • Advertising ID: per Google AdMob's policy

7. Your Rights

You may freely exercise the following rights:

  • Delete Data: clear all draw history via Settings > Clear History in the App
  • Reset App: restart from onboarding via Settings > Reset App
  • Reset Advertising ID: reset at any time via your Android device's Settings > Google > Ads
  • Opt Out of Personalized Ads: use the same path above
  • Uninstall App: removing the App from your device permanently deletes all local data

8. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that such information has been collected, we will delete it immediately.

9. Security

We take the following measures to protect your data:

  • All network communication is encrypted with HTTPS (TLS)
  • Data stored on your device is only accessible within the Android app sandbox
  • No user data is transmitted to or stored on developer servers

10. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will notify you via an in-app notice or by updating this page. The updated policy takes effect from the date it is posted here.

11. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact:

  • Email: support@birdlab.net
  • In-App: Settings > Contact Us
