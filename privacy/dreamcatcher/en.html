Dreamcatcher — Privacy Policy

App: Dreamcatcher
Package: net.birdlab.dreamcatcher
Effective date: June 1, 2026

Dreamcatcher ("the App") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data the App handles.

1. Data collected

The App does not collect personally identifying information (name, email, phone). To serve ads, the Google AdMob SDK may collect:

  • Advertising ID — a resettable ad identifier (personalization/measurement)
  • Device identifiers — model, OS version, language
  • Approximate location — from IP address, for ad regionalization
  • App interaction & diagnostics — ad performance and error diagnostics

This data is collected and processed by Google AdMob and is not sent to the developer's servers.

2. Data not collected

  • Name, email, phone, address
  • Precise GPS location
  • Photos, videos, contacts, calendar
  • Microphone audio, health, financial data
  • Account info (the App has no login/accounts)

3. Data stored on your device

These settings are stored only on your device and not transmitted:

  • Selected sounds, scenes, dreamcatcher, volume
  • Language, unlocked scenes
  • Onboarding state, app-open count (for review prompts)

4. Audio downloads

Some sounds are downloaded on demand from our content host (GitHub Releases). These requests contain no personal information.

5. Permissions

The App uses foreground-service / media playback and wake-lock permissions only to keep audio playing. It does not use the microphone, camera, contacts, or precise location.

6. Third parties

Ads are served by Google AdMob, which independently processes the data in section 1. Google Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy; partner data use: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

7. Your choices

You can reset your Advertising ID or limit ad personalization in your device settings (Settings → Privacy → Ads). Uninstalling the App removes all on-device data.

8. Children (COPPA / Google Play Families)

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Security

All network communication is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS).

10. Changes

We may update this policy; the effective date will be revised accordingly.

11. Contact

Birdlab — support@birdlab.net
