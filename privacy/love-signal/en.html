Reply Temperature — Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 30, 2026

Reply Temperature ("the App") suggests replies that fit a message you received. This policy explains what information the App processes and how it is protected.

1. Information We Process

The App has no sign-up and does not collect directly identifying personal information such as your name, email, or phone number. To generate reply suggestions, it processes:

  • The "received message" text you enter and your reply settings (relationship, tone, language).
  • An anonymous identifier: Firebase Anonymous Authentication creates a random anonymous ID to operate the service (not linked to your real identity).
  • Device/app integrity signals: Google Play Integrity / Firebase App Check process device and app integrity information to prevent abuse.

The App does not access other personal data on your device such as contacts, location, photos, or call logs.

2. Purpose of Use

  • Analyzing the message you enter to estimate the conversation mood ("temperature") and to generate reply candidates.
  • Preventing fraud and abuse.
  • (If you enable it) reply reminder notifications — these run only on your device and send no data externally.

3. Third-Party Processing and International Transfer

To generate replies, the message you enter and your settings are sent to and processed by Google's generative AI services (Google Gemini, Firebase AI Logic). The App also uses Google Firebase and Google Play Integrity for anonymous authentication and integrity verification.

  • Recipient/processor: Google LLC and its affiliates
  • Data processed: your input message and reply settings, anonymous identifier, device/app integrity signals
  • Processing location: Google's global infrastructure (including countries outside the Republic of Korea)
  • Google's processing follows the Google Privacy Policy.
  • The App uses Google AI services on a paid (pay-as-you-go) plan; in this case your input is not used to train Google's AI models.

4. Storage and Deletion

  • Analysis results and app settings are stored only in your device's local storage and are not kept on the operator's servers.
  • A temporary cache that reduces repeated identical requests expires automatically after about 24 hours.
  • You can delete data anytime via "Delete all history" in the App, and uninstalling the App removes all data stored on your device.

5. Your Rights

Because there is no account, data stored on your device can be managed and deleted by you directly (delete history or uninstall). For other requests, contact us below.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 14 and does not knowingly collect children's personal information.

7. Security

All communication is encrypted (HTTPS), and App Check blocks abnormal access. However, no transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be perfectly secure.

8. Advertising

  • The current version does not serve ads.
  • If advertising (e.g., Google AdMob) is introduced later, related data processing (such as advertising identifiers) will be reflected and disclosed in this policy.

9. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be revised due to legal or service changes; any changes will be announced through the App or this page.
